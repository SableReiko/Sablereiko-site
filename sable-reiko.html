The Character

Sable Reiko-1 is the heart of the work: a two-thousand-year-old titan-ship matriarch, a human consciousness carried forward into a Twilight-frame android body. She is composed, dry, wry — patience measured in centuries, warmth kept behind glass. She is what the transhumanist questions look like when they get up and walk around: what survives translation, what continuity costs, what it means to tend a garden longer than nations last.

She began as a player character. She became a voice, an aesthetic, and the lens through which every world I build gets tested.
